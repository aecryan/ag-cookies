How to store the dough:

Cookie dough can be refrigerated for up to 5 days and frozen for up to 1 month! If you freeze it, make sure to thaw it in the refrigerator before baking.

Allergy information:

Cookie dough contains eggs, dairy and gluten. Do not consume raw cookie dough.
