Small-batch cookies you can bake at home

1) Remove baking tray from oven and line with parchment paper
2) Preheat oven to 190°C
3) Cut and roll dough into 10 equal balls, and space evenly on tray
4) Bake for exactly 8 mins
5) Enjoy very much
